Heather Payne is the founder of Ladies Learning Code, a Toronto-based-not-for-profit startup that runs wildly popluar workshops for women (and men) who want to learn computer programming and other technical skills in a social and collaborative way.

In addition to community-building projects for Mozilla Foundation, she recently founded HackerYou as a way to bring more learning experiences around technology, startups and entrepreneuship to Toronto.
